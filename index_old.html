Jason Lister



As the Director of Client Reporting, Jason supervises the reporting staff in the daily collection and reconciliation of portfolio data, as well as the presentation of monthly and quarterly reports for our clients. Jason also supervises the client operations team in the daily matters involved in carrying out consultant and client instructions, such as account openings and changes, transactions and cash transfers. His role includes oversight of data and other reporting functions necessary for the operations of the firm.

Prior to joining Altair, Jason was a Vice President in the Operations department for Morgan Stanley & Co., where he was responsible for all aspects of performance evaluation for institutional and ultra-high net worth portfolios, as well as for managing a team of performance analysts who monitored asset allocations and validated investment returns for over 10,000 portfolios.

Prior to Morgan Stanley Jason spent over 2 year in Campulung Moldovenesc, Romania as a volunteer in the US Peace Corps. During his time abroad, he taught high school english, business english, and raised funds for the creation of a high school english library. .
